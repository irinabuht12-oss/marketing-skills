## What to Do

### For Meta/Facebook Ads
1. Go to Meta Ad Library (facebook.com/ads/library)
2. Search for each competitor
3. Look for ads started in the last 7 days
4. Save: ad image, headline, primary text, CTA

### For Google Ads
1. Use Google Ads Transparency Center
2. Search for each competitor
3. Look for new ads in the last 7 days
4. Save: headlines, descriptions
