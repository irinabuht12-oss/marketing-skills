Send me a full weekly report every Monday morning.

## When to Run

| Metric | This Week | Last Week | Change |
|--------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Spend | $X | $X | +X% |
| Clicks | X | X | +X% |
| Conversions | X | X | +X% |
| CPA | $X | $X | +X% |
| ROAS | X.X | X.X | +X% |

### 2. Top 5 Campaigns by Conversions
List with: name, spend, conversions, CPA

### 3. Bottom 5 Campaigns by CPA
List with: name, spend, conversions, CPA

### 4. Budget Pacing
- Monthly budget: $X
- Spent so far: $X (X% of month done)
- On track: Yes/No
- Projected end-of-month spend: $X

### 5. Quick Wins
Look at the data and suggest 2-3 things I could do:
- Campaigns to increase budget
- Campaigns to pause or reduce
- Keywords to add or remove
