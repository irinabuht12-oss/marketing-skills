# Search Term Mining

## Purpose
Find valuable new keywords and wasted spend from search terms report.

## When to Run
Every Monday and Thursday at 11:00 AM

## What to Do

### Step 1: Pull Search Terms
Get all search terms from the last 7 days with:
- At least 10 clicks OR
- At least $10 spend

### Step 2: Find Opportunities

**New Keywords to Add:**
Search terms that:
- Have 2+ conversions
- Not already exact match keywords
- Relevant to what we sell

**Negative Keywords to Add:**
Search terms that:
- Spent $20+ with zero conversions OR
- Clearly irrelevant (wrong intent, wrong product, competitors)
